Picnic of Love

Picnic of Love is the fifth album by Anal Cunt. It was released on July 21, 1998. The album was released as a joke and a parody of love songs, as well as of the band itself. The album is characterized by the opposite of everything that the band represents.

Fuckin' A

Fuckin' A is the eighth and final full-length album by Anal Cunt. It was released digitally on August 10, 2010, and then physically on January 11, 2011. The album is intended to be a parody of "cock rock" bands such as Motley Crue and Buckcherry and its cover uses the same image as Motley Crue's debut album "Too Fast for Love".
